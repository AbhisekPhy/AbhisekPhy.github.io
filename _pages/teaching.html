I am currently teaching the following course at IIT Gandhinagar:
  • Spring 2025: Advanced Condensed Matter Physics (PH 691-IV)
    • The course introduces quantum many-body theory and discusses advanced topics in condensed matter physics, useful for both theoretical and experimental research in the field.
    • Course Outline – Second Quantization, Jellium model, Hartree-Fock theory, Hubbard model and Ferromagnetism, Heisenberg model and superexchange, Landau Fermi liquid theory, BCS theory of superconductivity, Interacting bosons and superfluidity, Introduction to Quantum Hall Effetcs, Term-paper presentations with some advanced topics
